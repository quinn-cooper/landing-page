My Origin

Hi there, my name is Quinn! I was raised in Victoria, BC and I've been living in Vancouver for the past 8 years. I’m a Front-End Web Development student at BCIT who strives to create user-friendly, accessible, and memorable sites.

In my free time, I enjoy reading, writing, cooking, cross-stitching, and needle felting. I’m also drawn to horror, which fuels my creativity—but it’s not a limitation to what I can create. My goal is to create memorable designs.
